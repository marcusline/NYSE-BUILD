NYSE BUILD

NYSE BUILD is a website all about building your dream computer. You will be able to pick from vast majority of computer parts and put them all together. You're able to edit your build however you like! From adding more graphics cards to removing a computer part that dosen't fit.

Creators

The creators of NYSE BUILD are Marcus Seidefors and Elias Nywall. The aim of the website was to push the limit of the knowledge and possiblites of building a website based on computer parts. The goal was also find out and study what it takes to build a website.
